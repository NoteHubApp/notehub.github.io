Building the future of productivity and collaboration.

NoteHub is working to build the future of productivity and collaboration. We are currently working on an app, which is currently under development.

What can this app do?

In this app you can share notes, and manage your notes. Notes would support images, custom styling by our text editor, they also feature version control along with ability to invite people to collaborate with you!

When will this launch?

NoteHub is commited to developing this app, and get it shipped to users as soon as possible. We believe this is the future of productivity and collaboration. We expect to complete a functional app by the end of september, but it might take longer, but we'll get to you once its done!
